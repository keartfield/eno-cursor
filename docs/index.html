Eno Cursor

via: ENO (2024) Trailer

Inspired by Brian Eno's custom cursor from the 2024 documentary 'Eno' by Gary Hustwit

A gentle Mac app that helps you find your cursor with beautiful, customizable circles. Perfect for elderly users and those with visual impairments.

Download for Mac
v0.1.0 • 86.7 MB • All releases

Features

  • Dual-circle design that follows your cursor
  • Customizable colors for both circles
  • Adjustable sizes (100px to 2000px)
  • Runs quietly in your menu bar
  • Multi-display support

Installation

Download the DMG file, open it, and drag the app to your Applications folder. Launch from Applications or Spotlight.

Security Warning

If macOS shows "cannot verify developer", go to System Settings → Privacy & Security and click "Open Anyway". This app is safe but not signed with an expensive Apple certificate.

System Requirements

  • macOS 10.14 or later
  • Intel or Apple Silicon Mac
  • 4 GB RAM minimum
  • 100 MB available storage

How to Use

After installation, look for the ◎ icon in your menu bar. Click it to start/stop the cursor overlay. Access settings to customize colors and sizes to your preference.
